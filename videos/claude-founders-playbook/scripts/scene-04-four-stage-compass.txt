So Anthropic remapped startup building into four stages. Idea. M V P. Launch. Scale. Each one has a goal, an exit test, and a failure mode. Idea is finding a problem worth paying for. M V P is proving people actually will. Launch is going from one founder to a small team. And Scale is when every decision still going through you stops being an asset, and starts being the bottleneck.
