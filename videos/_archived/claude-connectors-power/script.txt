There is a feature in Claude that most users never turn on — and it's the most powerful thing Anthropic shipped this year.

It's called Connectors. And right now, 375 integrations are sitting in your settings, waiting.

Without Connectors, Claude only knows what you paste in. With them, Claude reads your Google Drive, scans your Gmail, browses your GitHub repositories, and checks your Slack messages — in real time, without you copying a single thing.

Here is how it works. Claude Connectors run on M C P — the Model Context Protocol — an open standard that lets Claude reach into your tools through a secure, permission-controlled tunnel. You authorize once, and Claude can pull the data whenever you ask. Your files never leave your accounts. Claude only reads what you explicitly ask it to look at in that conversation.

Now let's talk about the three workflows that actually change your daily work.

Workflow one: document synthesis. You have a project scattered across five Drive files, three emails, and a Slack thread. Normally you'd spend 30 minutes copying context into a chat window. With Google Drive and Gmail Connectors running, you describe the project in one sentence and Claude surfaces the relevant documents, extracts the key decisions, and delivers a summary. What took 30 minutes takes 90 seconds.

Workflow two: code review with full context. Connect GitHub and ask Claude to review a pull request. Claude reads the diff, traces the functions back through the repository, checks whether the implementation matches the comments in the issue, and flags anything inconsistent. No repository cloning. No pasting diffs. Full codebase context, available in the conversation.

Workflow three: background agents. This is the newest and least-known part. Claude Managed Agents — launched in April 2026 — let Connectors run in the background. You describe a task: monitor this Slack channel, summarize every GitHub issue that comes in this week, flag any Drive document that mentions a specific client. Claude runs that as a long-lived agent, not just a one-shot chat. You get results delivered to you.

To enable all of this: open claude.ai, go to Settings, click Connectors, and browse the directory. Connect Google Workspace, GitHub, and Slack in under two minutes. Then open any chat, tap the plus icon, and toggle the connectors for that session.
