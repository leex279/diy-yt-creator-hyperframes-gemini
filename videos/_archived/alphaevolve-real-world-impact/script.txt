Google DeepMind built an AI that does it differently. It's called Alpha-Evolve. It's not a chatbot. It writes code, tests its own code, and only keeps what works. Like evolution, but for algorithms. In one year, it's quietly improved more than a dozen.

Here's how good it is. For fifty-three years, the fastest known way to multiply two matrices used forty-nine multiplications. The record stood since nineteen sixty-nine.
