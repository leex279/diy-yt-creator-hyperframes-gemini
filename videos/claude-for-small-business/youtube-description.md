Key Concepts:
- Claude Cowork: Anthropic's collaborative workspace where small business teams chat with Claude, run agentic workflows, and connect their existing SaaS stack. Claude for Small Business ships these 15 workflows live inside Cowork.
- Claude Skills: reusable agentic capabilities (invoice chaser, margin analyzer, month-end prepper, etc.) that operators wire into their own workflows. The 15 skills launched here are pre-built; teams build their own on top.
- AI workflows for SMB: the bundle is what's new, not the model. Anthropic's bet is that 15-50 person companies don't want a chatbot — they want a pre-wired worker that already knows how to run payroll, close the books, and chase invoices.
- The 15-50 person SMB sweet spot: explicitly named by Lina Ochman as the target — HVAC companies, landscapers, real estate brokerages, e-commerce brands. Not solopreneurs (covered separately by the Solopreneurship Accelerator) and not the Fortune 500.
- Approval-required execution: every workflow generates a plan that you approve before Claude executes. Inherited permissions mean Claude can't access more than your underlying tool logins allow. Closes the 50%-data-security-hesitation gap.
