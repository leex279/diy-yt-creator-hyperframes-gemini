## Outlier Analysis (`claude for small business` shorts)

The "claude for small business" outliers don't show direct competitors on the
launch topic (Anthropic shipped May 13, 2026 — the window is fresh). Top
outlier framings worth borrowing:

| Title (paraphrased) | Outlier Score | Framing Pattern |
| --- | --- | --- |
| "Claude skills are a super power" | 32 | Bold-claim + product-name |
| "Claude Full Stack tool setup" | 50 | Tool-setup how-to (broader topic) |
| "INSANE Secret Million Dollar Claude Business Idea" | 4 | Stat-+-superlative hook |
| "7 claude hacks to level up" | 10 | Numbered-list + level-up frame |
| "Claude + [tool] = [outcome]" | varied | X + Y = Z formula |

**Lesson**: high-outlier short-form Claude content leads with **bold-claim** or
**numbered-list** frames. Our description should mirror this — front-load
"15 workflows + 15 skills" (number-anchored) as the visible promise.

   comparison-search intent.

   workflows, the 15-50 person SMB sweet spot, approval-required execution.
