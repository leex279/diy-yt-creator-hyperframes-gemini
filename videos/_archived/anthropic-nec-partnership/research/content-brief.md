## Core Value Proposition
Anthropic helps NEC — Japan's oldest tech giant — turn 30,000 employees into an AI-native engineering org by deploying Claude and Claude Code across the company and into their flagship BluStellar Scenario consulting program.

**Primary**: Developers and technical audience watching enterprise-AI moves; people who care about Claude Code adoption signals at scale.
