Here's what was true until June 15. Third-party autonomous agents like OpenClaw weren't allowed to authenticate through your Claude subscription. Separate API setup. Separately billed. Dev harnesses like Archon — which just wrap your Claude Code workflow — sat in a gray zone. And your own Agent SDK scripts? Those counted against the same usage limit as your editor work.

Starting June 15, Anthropic clears the rules. A new monthly programmatic credit lands on every paid plan, separate from your editor budget. And third-party Agent SDK tools — including the previously-banned ones — can now officially authenticate through your Claude plan and draw from this credit pool.
